Microvessel Chaste

A Chaste plug-in library for 3D spatial modelling of vascularized tissue, including discrete cells and vessels, PDE solvers, meshing and geometry tools.

Learn More

 

Network Analysis

Generate artificial vessel networks and analyse real and artificial network characteristics.

Blood Flow

One-dimensional models of blood flow, non-linear rheology, haematocrit splitting and flow mediated structural adaptation.

Chemical Transport

Reaction-diffusion equations for nutrient, growth factor and drug transport with discrete sinks and sources at cells and vessels.

Angiogenesis

Sprouting angiogenesis using lattice and off-lattice models.

Discrete Cells

Cell migration, proliferation and cell cycle progression based on sub-cellular ODE models.

Geometry and Meshing

Simple geometry manipulation using piece-wise linear complexes. STL reading and writing. Meshing for finite element analysis.
